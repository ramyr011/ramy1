Company Overview

Yara International Recruitment - A Visionary Approach to Recruitment and Documentation

Yara International Recruitment - A leading institution in the field of Egyptian labor export since 1998. We take pride in being licensed by the Egyptian Ministry of Manpower under license number 387, and we boast over a quarter-century of experience in providing distinguished and reliable recruitment services. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism throughout the recruitment process, and we strive to build bridges of success between job seekers and employers around the world.

We offer comprehensive solutions in the field of immigration and visas, including Net booking services for various visits: family visits, residence extensions, and return services.
